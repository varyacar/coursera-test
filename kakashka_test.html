Levelled Texts (A2–B2):
Explore short, interesting texts carefully crafted for your level. From myths and history to everyday life and science, each text helps you improve reading, vocabulary, and grammar naturally. Every story is engaging and thought-provoking, so learning feels like an adventure.
Topic-Based Texts (A0–B2):
Dive into longer, topic-focused texts with wider vocabulary coverage. Each text includes a useful wordlist and exercises to help you understand and remember new words. Topics range from travel and food to culture and nature, making reading both fun and educational.
Koala Dialogues & Mini-Stories:
Follow cute koalas as they chat and explore small adventures. Dialogues are level-appropriate and introduce new expressions in context. Complete the text exercises to unlock extra challenges or mini-games with the koalas, making learning playful and memorable.
Personalize Your Koala & Your Site:
Make your learning space truly yours! After finishing a text, choose a theme to decorate your site — from pyramids for history texts to ocean waves for sea stories. Track progress, unlock achievements, and customize your koala’s home, creating a unique experience every step of the way.
